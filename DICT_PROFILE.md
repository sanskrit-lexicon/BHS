## When to use BHS

Use BHS for **Buddhist Sanskrit** vocabulary — words found in Mahāyāna texts that MW marks as `[BHSkt.]` or that are absent from MW entirely. For Classical Sanskrit, prefer MW + PWG. BHS and MW are complementary, not competing, references.

---

## Relationship to MW

BHS (Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit) is a specialized dictionary for the hybrid Sanskrit of Buddhist canonical texts. Unlike the pan-Sanskrit dicts (MW, AP90), [Edgerton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Franklin_Edgerton)'s BHS focuses on vocabulary found in Buddhist Hybrid Sanskrit texts (Mahāvastu, Lalitavistara, etc.) that is not well-attested in Classical Sanskrit dictionaries. An essential supplement to MW for Buddhist scholars.
